Wind Turbines

Where does the data come from?

This data is from the CORGIS Dataset Project. The data contains statistics about wind turbines

Wind Turbine Questions

Why does the height of the wind turbine matter?

Wind turbines with lower heights aren't always as good as taller ones because shorter turbines can have their wind blocked by trees or manmade structures

What makes an area a good place for a wind turbine?

A flat plane with lots of wind or a mountainside that is hit by wind a lot

What is a utility scale wind turbine?

A utility scale turbine is a wind turbine that is used for bigger purposes than personal use. All of the turbines in this dataset are utility scale
